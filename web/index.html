Scam Warning

We have received reports of people being contacted by text message from the number 778-654-1116 claiming to be UBC Parking informing them they have a ticket. This is a reported scam and you should not follow any links in these text messages. UBC Parking will never text people about outstanding tickets, and instead will always email and direct parking users to check their account on our website.

Key Project

Forestry Smart Access Project

We're currently working with UBC Faculty of Forestry to enhance accessibility throughout the building with heightened security, auditability and balancing convenience. By the end of the project, users of the building will benefit from an increase of card access controlled doors, intelligent key management through the deployment and use of key lockboxes, and security technology deployments.
